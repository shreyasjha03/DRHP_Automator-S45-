## Resolutions Passed

### Special Resolution: Increase in Authorized Share Capital with Preference Shares

**RESOLVED** that the Authorized Share Capital of the company be increased from Rs. 2,00,000 (Rupees Two Lakhs) divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each to **Rs. 3,00,000 (Rupees Three Lakhs) divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each and 10,000 Cumulative Redeemable Preference Shares of Rs. 10 each.**

#### Capital Details:
- **Previous Authorized Capital:** Rs. 2,00,000
  - 20,000 Equity Shares @ Rs. 10 per share
  
- **New Authorized Capital:** Rs. 3,00,000
  - 20,000 Equity Shares @ Rs. 10 per share
  - 10,000 Preference Shares @ Rs. 10 per share

#### Capital Composition:
- Equity Capital: Rs. 2,00,000 (66.67%)
- Preference Capital: Rs. 1,00,000 (33.33%)

#### Voting Results:
- In Favour: 90% of voting members present
- Against: 10%
- **RESOLUTION PASSED**

This resolution shall be effective from 22/07/2021.
