# Minutes of Extra-ordinary General Meeting

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Date of Meeting:** 17/11/2016
**Time:** 2:00 PM IST
**Venue:** Registered Office

## Attendance
- 6 members present (in person or through proxy)
- Constituting 85% of the total shareholding

## Resolutions Passed

### Special Resolution 1: Increase in Authorized Share Capital

**RESOLVED** that the Authorized Share Capital of the company be increased from Rs. 1,00,000 (Rupees One Lakh) divided into 10,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each to **Rs. 2,00,000 (Rupees Two Lakhs) divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each.**

#### Capital Details:
- **Previous Authorized Capital:** Rs. 1,00,000 (Rs. 100,000)
  - 10,000 Equity Shares @ Rs. 10 per share
  
- **New Authorized Capital:** Rs. 2,00,000 (Rs. 200,000)
  - 20,000 Equity Shares @ Rs. 10 per share

#### Voting Results:
- In Favour: 85% of voting members present
- Against: 15%
- **RESOLUTION PASSED**

This resolution shall be effective from 17/11/2016.
