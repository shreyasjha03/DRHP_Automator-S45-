# Board Resolution - Approval of Capital Increase with Preference Shares

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Date of Board Meeting:** 15/07/2021

## Resolution: Approval of Increase in Authorized Share Capital (Including Preference Shares)

The Board of Directors of Apex Corporation Private Limited, at their meeting held on 15/07/2021, hereby approves the proposal to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the company by introducing Preference Shares and recommend the same to the members for approval at the Extra-ordinary General Meeting.

### Current Authorized Capital (As on 15/07/2021)
**Authorized Share Capital:** Rs. 2,00,000 (Rupees Two Lakhs)
- Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each
- Face Value per Share: Rs. 10

### Proposed Authorized Capital
**New Authorized Share Capital:** Rs. 3,00,000 (Rupees Three Lakhs)
- Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each
- AND 10,000 Cumulative Redeemable Preference Shares of Rs. 10 each

### Capital Structure Change
- Old Capital: Rs. 2,00,000 (20,000 equity shares)
- New Capital: Rs. 3,00,000 (20,000 equity + 10,000 preference shares)
- Increase: Rs. 1,00,000 (50% increase)

### Details
- Equity Shares: 20,000 × Rs. 10 = Rs. 2,00,000 (unchanged)
- Preference Shares: 10,000 × Rs. 10 = Rs. 1,00,000 (new)
- Total Authorized: Rs. 3,00,000

Passed on 15/07/2021
