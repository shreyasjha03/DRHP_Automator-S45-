# Amended Memorandum of Association

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Amendment Effective Date:** 17/11/2016

## Capital Clause (As Amended)

### Article 2: Authorized Share Capital

The authorized share capital of the company is hereby amended and revised as under:

#### Previous Authorized Capital (Up to 16/11/2016)
- Rupees One Lakh (Rs. 1,00,000) only
- Divided into 10,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each

#### New Authorized Capital (From 17/11/2016)
- **Rupees Two Lakhs (Rs. 2,00,000) only**
- **Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each**

The authorized share capital represents the maximum capital the company can raise through the issuance of shares without further amendment to the Memorandum of Association.

### Effective Date
This amendment has been approved by the members in EGM held on 17/11/2016 and is effective from 17/11/2016.

The new capital structure:
- From: Rs. 1,00,000 into 10,000 equity shares
- To: Rs. 2,00,000 into 20,000 equity shares
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share (unchanged)

Amendment Approved: 17/11/2016
