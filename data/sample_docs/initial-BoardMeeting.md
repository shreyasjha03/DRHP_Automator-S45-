# Board Resolution

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Date of Board Meeting:** 01/01/2015

## Resolutions Passed

### Resolution 1: Approval of Authorized Share Capital

The Board of Directors of Apex Corporation Private Limited, at their meeting held on 01/01/2015, hereby approves and confirms the Authorized Share Capital of the company as follows:

**Existing Authorized Capital:** Nil (At Incorporation)

**Authorized Share Capital Structure:**
- Rupees One Lakh (Rs. 1,00,000) only
- Divided into 10,000 (ten thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10 (ten rupees) each
- Total Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000

This structure was approved during incorporation and forms the basis of the company's capital structure.

Approved by Board of Directors on 01/01/2015.
