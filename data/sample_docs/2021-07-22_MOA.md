# Amended Memorandum of Association

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Amendment Effective Date:** 22/07/2021

## Capital Clause (As Amended)

### Article 2: Authorized Share Capital

The authorized share capital of the company is hereby amended and revised as under:

#### Previous Authorized Capital (Up to 21/07/2021)
- Rupees Two Lakhs (Rs. 2,00,000) only
- Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each

#### New Authorized Capital (From 22/07/2021)
- **Rupees Three Lakhs (Rs. 3,00,000) only**
- **Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each**
- **AND 10,000 Cumulative Redeemable Preference Shares of Rs. 10 each**

### Capital Composition
- Equity Shares: 20,000 @ Rs. 10 = Rs. 2,00,000
- Preference Shares: 10,000 @ Rs. 10 = Rs. 1,00,000
- Total Authorized Capital: Rs. 3,00,000

### Effective Date
This amendment has been approved by the members in EGM held on 22/07/2021 and is effective from 22/07/2021.

The new capital structure:
- From: Rs. 2,00,000 (20,000 equity shares)
- To: Rs. 3,00,000 (20,000 equity + 10,000 preference shares)
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share (for both types)

Amendment Approved: 22/07/2021
