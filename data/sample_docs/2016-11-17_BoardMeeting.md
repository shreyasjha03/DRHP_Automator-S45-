# Board Resolution - Approval of Capital Increase

**Company:** Apex Corporation Private Limited
**Date of Board Meeting:** 10/11/2016

## Resolution: Approval of Increase in Authorized Share Capital

The Board of Directors of Apex Corporation Private Limited, at their meeting held on 10/11/2016, hereby approves the proposal to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the company and recommend the same to the members for approval at the Extra-ordinary General Meeting.

### Current Authorized Capital (As on 10/11/2016)
**Authorized Share Capital:** Rs. 1,00,000 (Rupees One Lakh)
- Divided into 10,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each
- Face Value per Share: Rs. 10

### Proposed Authorized Capital
**New Authorized Share Capital:** Rs. 2,00,000 (Rupees Two Lakhs)
- Divided into 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10 each
- Face Value per Share: Rs. 10

### Reason for Increase
The increase in authorized capital is necessitated to meet the growing financial requirements and operational needs of the company.

### Capital Structure Change
- Old Capital: Rs. 1,00,000 from 10,000 shares
- New Capital: Rs. 2,00,000 from 20,000 shares
- Increase: Rs. 1,00,000 (100% increase)

The Board recommends this proposal for approval by members through postal ballot and EGM.

Passed on 10/11/2016
